# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in communication.
- Assume good intent and focus on technical outcomes.
- Accept feedback gracefully and collaborate in good faith.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments.
- Public or private conduct that harms a safe community environment.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

To report unacceptable behavior, open a private security-style report as described in [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md), or contact the maintainers through repository channels.
